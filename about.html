About Us

Did you know that according to the Federal government, there are currently over 160,000 companies in Alberta – 98% of our entire workforce! – classed as “small” businesses?

These are companies that are run with fewer staff, less capital, and unique financial needs.

Additionally, according to 2021 statistics compiled by the Business Council of Alberta, 87% of these small businesses have fewer than 5 employees. That means we’re a province of small businesses fueling contractors, who are their own small businesses. What a great system!

At Western Commercial Finance, we understand. We are one of those small businesses, and we know the value and importance of ensuring that our fellow Albertans and their businesses are handled with care, and their needs met even when conventional lending is not available.
